"Host" is the IP address or a hostname of the node; "Port" is the TCP port the node is responding at; "CPUs" is the total amount of processors at the particular node; "Threads" is the amount of active threads vs. total threads at the node; "Score" is the trust score of the node it exposes currently; "Wallets" is the total amount of wallets the node is holding; "Version" is the version of software the node runs; "NScore" is the total network trust score the node estimates; "Remotes" is the total amount of remote nodes the node is connected to; "History" is the amount of recently processed PUSH requests; "Queue" is the amount of PUSH requests staying in the line and wating processing; "QAge" is the age of the oldest wallet in the queue, in seconds; "Speed" is the average amount of time in seconds the node spends per each PUSH request processing; "Age" is the amount of hours since recent reboot of the node.
