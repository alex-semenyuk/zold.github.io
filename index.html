Where to Get ZLD?

We don't sell ZLD. There are three possible ways to get it: 1) Fund any project in Zerocracy and get an equal amount of ZLD as a reward, 2) Join any of them as a programmer and receive ZLD for the tasks completed, or 3) Start your own Zold node and collect taxes and rewards.

Is this an ICO?

No, it's not. ZLD is not an investment instrument. It is an additional intrinsic motivator for those who are interested in joining the software development revolution, which Zerocracy is leading.

What Is the Roadmap?

Zold was created and launched by Yegor Bugayenko on May 27, 2018. The technical cap of the currency is 2.15b ZLD. There is a formula that restricts the speed of emission, allowing no greater than 50% of the total capacity to be issued before May, 2021; the remaining 50% can be issued before the end of 2028. However, ZLD will be given away strictly according to the rules mentioned above.
